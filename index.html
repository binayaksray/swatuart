Frequently Asked Questions

1. Are all artworks handmade?

Yes, 100% handmade 🤍
Every piece is handcrafted with Lippan art, clay, and mirrors. No machine prints. Each artwork is one-of-a-kind.

2. Can I customize size, color, or name?

Absolutely! I love custom orders ✨
Choose size from 10" to 24", color theme, name, date, or quote. Add details in "Custom Note" while ordering. Price depends on size & design.

3. How long for making & delivery?

Making: 7-10 working days + Delivery: 3-5 days 📦
All pieces are made fresh on order. Need it faster? Ask for "Express Making" with extra charges.

4. What materials do you use? Is it durable?

Premium MDF, clay, real mirrors, acrylic colors + varnish.
With care, artworks last 10+ years. Avoid direct sunlight & water. Varnish protects from dust.

5. What if my artwork is damaged?

100% Safe Packing Guarantee 📦
Packed with bubble wrap, thermocol & hard box. If damaged, send an unboxing video within 48 hours. Free replacement/refund, no questions.

6. Do you ship across India? Shipping charges?

Yes, Pan-India shipping 🇮🇳
Charges: ₹150-250 based on weight/location. FREE shipping on orders above ₹5000.

7. How do I clean the artwork?

Super easy ✨
Just dust with a dry soft cloth or brush. No water or chemicals. Varnish keeps colors safe.

8. Do you take bulk orders?

Yes! For weddings, housewarming, gifts 🎁
